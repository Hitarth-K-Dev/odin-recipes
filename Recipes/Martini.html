Martini Recipe

Martinis are amazing - especially if they're made the right way, and so is this recipe! Follow the steps to have an amazing drink!

RECIPE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 60 ml of your favourite vodka or gin
  • 15 ml dry vermouth (about a tablespoon ice lemon peel, or an olive to garnish)

STEPS:

  1. Fill your cocktail shaker or tall glass with ice, and add your two alcohols.
  2. Stir using a tall cocktail spoon for about 30 seconds.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass and serve with your chosen garnish.
  4. Yes, that's literally the entire recipe
  5. ENJOY!
