TACO Recipe!!!

Tacos are a universal comfort food (that's a fact btw) and its about time you learn how to make them! Follow the steps to make something easy, fast, and insanely delishious!!

RECIPE - should serve a small village, or one hungry person

INGREDIENTS:

  • Refried Beans
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tomato Sauce and Water
  • Tortillas
  • Taco Topping

STEPS:

  1. With your choice of toppings layed out, and a taco bar set up, go CRAZY! Have fun with what you put inside the tacos, dip in whatever you want to, make it a self service station if this is a party!.
  2. ENJOY!
